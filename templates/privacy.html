Non-Personal Information:

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We use a combination of industry-standard security measures such as encryption, firewalls, and secure server protocols to protect your data.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your data with trusted third-party companies who assist in operating our business, processing payments, or delivering services (e.g., shipping providers).
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, we may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or government requests.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your shopping experience and analyze website traffic. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences, improve functionality, and personalize content.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but doing so may affect your experience on our website.

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the data we have on file for you.
  • Correction: You can update or correct your personal information by contacting us.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information, though certain data may be retained for legal or administrative purposes.
  • Opt-out: You can opt out of marketing emails by following the unsubscribe link in any promotional communication.

Refund Policies

Refund and Return Policy

At Multiverse Clothing, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your item, we are here to help.

1. Returns

  • Eligibility: Returns are accepted on unworn, unwashed, and undamaged items within 30 days of delivery.
  • Condition: Items must have all original tags attached and be in the same condition as when received.
  • Non-returnable items: Sale items, gift cards, or personalized items are non-returnable.

2. Return Process

  • Contact Us: To initiate a return, contact us at multiverseclothings@gmail.com or through our contact page. Include your order number, a brief description of the issue, and the item(s) you wish to return.
  • Return Shipping: Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or there was an error on our part.
  • Packaging: Ensure items are securely packaged to avoid damage during return shipping.

3. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will process your refund. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method.

  • Processing Time: Please allow 7-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account after approval.

4. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you want a different size, color, or item, please return the original item and place a new order.

5. Defective or Damaged Items

If you received a defective or damaged item, contact us within 7 days of receiving the product. We will cover return shipping and offer a full refund or replacement, depending on your preference.

6. International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs. Refunds will be processed in the original currency, and conversion rates may apply.
